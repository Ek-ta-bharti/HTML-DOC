About Joint Pain

Joint pain is a common issue that affects many people. It can result from various conditions such as arthritis, injuries, or overuse. Understanding the root cause of your joint pain is crucial for effective treatment and relief.

Common symptoms include discomfort, swelling, and stiffness. Managing joint pain often involves a combination of treatments, including physical therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes.

Yoga has been shown to be highly effective in reducing joint pain and improving mobility. It helps strengthen the muscles around the joints, improve flexibility, and reduce inflammation.
